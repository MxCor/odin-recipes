Ratatouille

Description

A delicious French Provençal dish of stewed vegetables originating in Nice, brought to the attention of worldwide audiences with Disney Pixar's Ratatouille.

Ingredients

Vegetables

  • 2 Eggplants
  • 6 roma tomatoes
  • 2 yellow squashes
  • 2 courgettes

Sauce

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 red bell pepper, diced
  • 1 yellow bell pepper, diced
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 2 400g tins of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil

Herb Seasoning

  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons fresh thyme
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 4 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven for 375˚F (190˚C)
  2. Slice the eggplant, tomatoes, squash, and zucchini into approximately ¹⁄₁₆-inch (1-mm) rounds, then set aside
  3. Make the sauce: Heat the olive oil in a 12-inch (30-cm) oven-safe pan over medium-high heat the onion, garlic, and bell peppers until soft, about 10 minutes
  4. Season with salt and pepper, then add the crushed tomatoes. Stir until the ingredients are fully incorporated
  5. Remove from heat, then add the basil. Stir once more, then smooth the surface of the sauce with a spatula
  6. Arrange the sliced veggies in alternating patterns, (for example, eggplant, tomato, squash, zucchini) on top of the sauce from the outer edge to the middle of the pan. Season with salt and pepper
  7. Make the herb seasoning: In a small bowl, mix together the basil, garlic, parsley, thyme, salt, pepper, and olive oil. Spoon the herb seasoning over the vegetables
  8. Cover the pan with foil and bake for 40 minutes. Uncover, then bake for another 20 minutes, until the vegetables are softened
  9. Serve while hot as a main dish or side. The ratatouille is also excellent the next day--cover with foil and reheat in a 350˚F (180˚C) oven for 15 minutes, or simply microwave to desired temperature
